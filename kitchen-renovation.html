Kitchen Renovation in Los Angeles

Updating your kitchen with MTDT Renovation means creating a space that is both beautiful and functional. Our team helps you make the most of your layout, ensures quality workmanship, and offers expert guidance every step of the way.

We also incorporate practical solutions such as energy-efficient appliances and water-saving fixtures to help reduce utility costs and support sustainable living, without compromising comfort or style.

Our Kitchen Renovation Process

  • 1. Understanding Your Vision and Budget We start by listening to your ideas and defining a realistic plan that matches your needs, style, and budget. Our goal is to ensure your kitchen becomes both functional and beautiful without unnecessary expenses.

  • 2. Detailed Measurement & Layout Planning Our team measures your kitchen space and reviews the current layout. We then suggest smart design solutions — from L-shaped to open-plan concepts — to make the best use of your space.

  • 3. Material & Finish Selection We guide you through choosing flooring, cabinetry, countertops, and fixtures that match your style while ensuring durability and easy maintenance.

  • 4. Permits & Approvals If your project requires permits, we handle all the paperwork and coordinate with local authorities so the renovation fully complies with Los Angeles building codes.

  • 5. Safe Demolition We carefully remove old cabinets, tiles, and appliances while protecting your home from dust and damage.

  • 6. Foundation & Wall Preparation We prepare the base of your kitchen by installing drywall, leveling the floors, and ensuring all surfaces are ready for the next stage.

  • 7. Painting & Wall Finishes We apply high-quality, moisture-resistant paint or other wall finishes that are easy to clean and long-lasting.

  • 8. Cabinet, Countertop & Fixture Installation We install cabinets, countertops, sinks, and other fixtures with precision, ensuring perfect alignment and secure fittings.

  • 9. Appliance & Lighting Setup Our team installs and tests your appliances and sets up functional lighting — from ceiling fixtures to under-cabinet lights — for both style and practicality.

  • 10. Final Inspection & Clean-Up We go through every detail, fix any minor imperfections, clean the space thoroughly, and make sure your new kitchen is ready to enjoy from day one.

Our Kitchen Renovation Services

At MTDT Renovation, we deliver tailored kitchen renovation solutions that combine functionality, style, and durability. Our services include:

We assess your kitchen’s existing lighting and create a customized upgrade plan to enhance both function and atmosphere.

Options include energy-efficient LED under-cabinet lights to brighten countertops, recessed ceiling lighting for even illumination, and stylish pendant fixtures to add character and make your kitchen feel more spacious.

We offer modern technology integrations such as smart outlets, smart lighting, Wi-Fi-enabled appliances, and automated faucets—helping you create a convenient, efficient, and future-ready kitchen.

Our team designs and installs high-quality custom cabinets using durable materials such as MDF, solid wood, and plywood.

We maximize storage with deep drawers and functional layouts while ensuring the design complements your kitchen’s overall aesthetic.

From classic subway tiles to contemporary glass, mirror finishes, marble, and ceramic options—we install backsplashes that protect your walls from spills while adding a stylish focal point to your kitchen.

We provide custom-fitted countertops in quartz, butcher block, and granite that seamlessly integrate with your kitchen’s layout and cabinetry, bringing elegance and durability to the heart of your home.

Our flooring solutions are durable, easy to maintain, and safe.

Choose from ceramic and porcelain tiles, natural stone, engineered wood, or vinyl to match your kitchen design and lifestyle needs.

Types of Kitchens We Renovate

At MTDT Renovation, we bring style, functionality, and lasting quality to kitchens across Los Angeles. Here are the four most popular layouts our clients choose:

  • 1. L-Shaped Kitchens Two adjoining walls form an “L” layout, creating a smooth workflow between cooking, prep, and cleaning areas. Perfect for both small and large spaces, this design can easily include an island or breakfast bar for extra functionality.

  • 2. U-Shaped Kitchens Cabinetry and countertops run along three connected walls, creating a well-organized cooking space with maximum counter area. A favorite choice for homeowners who love to cook and entertain.

  • 3. Peninsula Kitchens We prepare the base of your kitchen by installing drywall, leveling the floors, and ensuring all surfaces are ready for the next stage.

  • 4. Open-Concept Kitchens Combining the kitchen with dining and living areas creates a bright, spacious feel. This style often features an island, upgraded flooring, and modern lighting to enhance the flow of the entire home.
