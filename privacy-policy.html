Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how MTDT Renovation, a home renovation and construction company based in Los Angeles, California, collects, uses, and shares your personal information. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and data gathered automatically during your use of our site.

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address (for project quotes and site visits).
  • Project Details: Information about your renovation needs, budget, material preferences, and property specifications.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent on pages, referral sources, and interactions with our site.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers (via Google Analytics, etc.).

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our services (e.g., providing quotes, scheduling consultations, executing renovation projects).
  • To communicate with you regarding project updates, service inquiries, and promotional offers.
  • To improve our website and services by analyzing user behavior and preferences.
  • To comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that perform services on our behalf, such as specialized contractors, IT hosting, and analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to respond to valid legal processes.

4. Your Rights (California Residents - CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: The right to request what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • Right to Delete: The right to request the deletion of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (note: we do not sell your data).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: The right not to be discriminated against for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, track usage, and analyze marketing effectiveness. You can control or refuse cookies through your browser settings.
